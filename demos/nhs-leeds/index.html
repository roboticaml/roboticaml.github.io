Breathing Control - Information for patients.

Here are some breathing exercises to help you relax.

Make sure you are sat in a comfortable position.

Breath in through your nose and out of your mouth.

You breath in and count to one.

Breath out and count to do two in your head and stop.

You must try to relax your muscles when you are doing your breathing exercises.
